MINIMISE NATIONAL EXAMS LEAKAGE

Our service charter

The Kenya National Council is dedicated to offering excellent services.

Our main aim is to reduce exam leakage during transportation.

Here instead of having the exams being trasported, the exams are to be sent to every registered school that is Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) for the Primary Schools and Kenya Secondary School Education (KCSE), for Secondary Schools.

After sending they are to be downloaded and issued to the students after which they are to start their National Exams.
